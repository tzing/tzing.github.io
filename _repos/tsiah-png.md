Tsia̍h-Pn̄g is a group buying tool for collecting orders (especially the meal/drink orders) from your friends.

Tsia̍h-Pn̄g is the word for eat food in Taiwanese Hokkien. I first created this project because I always collect breakfast orders from colleagues.
